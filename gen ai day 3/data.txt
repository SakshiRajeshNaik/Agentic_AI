Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. AI systems can perform tasks such as problem-solving, decision-making, speech recognition, and language translation.

Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that allows computers to learn from data without being explicitly programmed. It uses algorithms to identify patterns and make predictions.

Deep Learning is a specialized area of machine learning that uses artificial neural networks with multiple layers. It is widely used in image recognition, natural language processing, and speech recognition.

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of AI that focuses on the interaction between computers and human language. It enables applications like chatbots, translation systems, and voice assistants.

Generative AI refers to models that can generate new content such as text, images, audio, and code. Examples include large language models and image generation systems.
